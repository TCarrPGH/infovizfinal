Abstract

Whooping Cough(pertussis)is a highly contagious airborne virus that causes extreme coughing that can cause breathing problems and can be fatal. Occurance of infection has decreased decreased since the invention of the vaccine in the 1940s, however a spike in reported cases has increased since the late 90's.
